# Lesson 10 Sentences

## 必须掌握

1. Stand up.
2. Sit down.
3. Jump.
4. Run.
5. Walk slowly.
6. Stop.
7. Turn around.
8. Clap your hands.
9. Stomp your feet.
10. Open your arms.
11. Close your eyes.
12. Touch the floor.
13. Dance with me.
14. Come here.
15. Go there.
16. Good moving.

## 可扩展

1. Jump high.
2. Walk fast.
3. Spin around.
4. Freeze.
5. Move like a robot.
6. Move like a bunny.

## 替换练习

- Stand up. / Sit down.
- Jump. / Run. / Walk slowly.
- Come here. / Go there.
- Open your arms. / Close your eyes.
