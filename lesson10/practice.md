# Lesson 10 Practice

## Game 1: Action Race

家长快速发指令：

- Stand up.
- Sit down.
- Jump.
- Stop.

## Game 2: Slow and Fast

练习不同动作节奏：

- Walk slowly.
- Walk fast.
- Freeze.

## Game 3: Go Here, Go There

在房间两端做游戏：

- Come here.
- Go there.
- Turn around.

## Game 4: Animal Moves

模仿动作：

- Move like a robot.
- Move like a bunny.
- Jump high.

## Game 5: Dance and Freeze

放音乐做游戏：

- Dance with me.
- Stop.
- Freeze.

## 两周建议

### Day 1 到 Day 3

只用下面 5 句：

- Stand up.
- Sit down.
- Jump.
- Stop.
- Good moving.

### Day 4 到 Day 7

增加 5 句：

- Run.
- Walk slowly.
- Turn around.
- Come here.
- Go there.

### Day 8 到 Day 14

加入问答和替换：

- Clap your hands.
- Stomp your feet.
- Open your arms.
- Touch the floor.
- Dance with me.
