# Lesson 4 Sentences

## 必须掌握

1. This is your shirt.
2. Put on your pants.
3. Put on your jacket.
4. Put on your hat.
5. Put on your socks.
6. Put on your shoes.
7. Take off your pajamas.
8. Which shirt do you want?
9. I want the blue shirt.
10. This one.
11. That one.
12. Zip up your jacket.
13. Button your coat.
14. Where is your backpack?
15. Here is your backpack.
16. We are going out.

## 可扩展

1. Is it too big?
2. It's too small.
3. Turn around.
4. Stand still.
5. You look great.
6. Let's go outside.

## 替换练习

- Put on your hat. / Put on your socks. / Put on your shoes.
- This one. / That one.
- Is it too big? / It's too small.
- Here is your backpack. / Here is your jacket.
