# Lesson 4 Practice

## Game 1: Dress the Teddy

给玩偶穿衣服：

- Put on your hat.
- Put on your socks.
- Put on your shoes.
- Zip up your jacket.

## Game 2: Which One

摆出两件衣服或两双鞋，练习：

- Which shirt do you want?
- This one.
- That one.

## Game 3: Find It

家长提问：

- Where is your backpack?
- Where are your shoes?

孩子找到后说 Here is your backpack. 或 Here they are.

## Game 4: Dress and Move

一边穿衣一边说：

- Turn around.
- Stand still.
- Put on your jacket.

## Game 5: Replace One Word

替换练习：

- Put on your jacket.
- Put on your hat.
- Put on your shoes.

## 两周建议

### Day 1 到 Day 3

只用下面 5 句：

- Put on your pants.
- Put on your jacket.
- Put on your shoes.
- This one.
- That one.

### Day 4 到 Day 7

增加 5 句：

- Take off your pajamas.
- Which shirt do you want?
- I want the blue shirt.
- Here is your backpack.
- We are going out.

### Day 8 到 Day 14

加入问答和替换：

- Put on your hat.
- Zip up your jacket.
- Button your coat.
- Turn around.
- You look great.
