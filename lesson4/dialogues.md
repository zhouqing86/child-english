# Lesson 4 Dialogues

## Dialogue 1

Parent: Take off your pajamas.

Child: Okay.

Parent: This is your shirt.

Child: Okay.

Parent: Put on your pants.

Child: Okay.

## Dialogue 2

Parent: Which shirt do you want?

Child: This one.

Parent: The blue shirt?

Child: Yes. I want the blue shirt.

## Dialogue 3

Parent: Put on your jacket.

Child: Okay.

Parent: Zip up your jacket.

Child: Help me.

Parent: Sure.

## Dialogue 4

Parent: Where is your backpack?

Child: Here is my backpack.

Parent: Put on your shoes.

Child: Okay.

Parent: We are going out.

Child: Let's go outside.
