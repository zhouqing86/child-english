# Lesson 7 Dialogues

## Dialogue 1

Parent: What color is it?

Child: It is red.

Parent: Good job.

## Dialogue 2

Parent: Show me the blue one.

Child: This one?

Parent: Yes, the blue one.

## Dialogue 3

Parent: What is this?

Child: This is a circle.

Parent: And this?

Child: This is a square.

## Dialogue 4

Parent: Find a red ball.

Child: Here it is.

Parent: Match the same color.

Child: Okay.
