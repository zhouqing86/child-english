# Lesson 11 Dialogues

## Dialogue 1

Parent: Are you happy?

Child: I'm happy.

Parent: Show me a happy face.

Child: Smile.

## Dialogue 2

Parent: Are you sad?

Child: I'm sad.

Parent: It's okay.

Child: I need a hug.

## Dialogue 3

Parent: Are you angry?

Child: I'm angry.

Parent: Take a deep breath.

Child: Okay.

## Dialogue 4

Parent: Are you tired?

Child: I'm tired.

Parent: Are you sleepy?

Child: I'm sleepy.
