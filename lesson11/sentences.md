# Lesson 11 Sentences

## 必须掌握

1. Are you happy?
2. I'm happy.
3. Are you sad?
4. I'm sad.
5. Are you angry?
6. I'm angry.
7. Are you scared?
8. I'm scared.
9. Are you tired?
10. I'm tired.
11. Are you sleepy?
12. I'm sleepy.
13. Don't cry.
14. Take a deep breath.
15. It's okay.
16. I need a hug.

## 可扩展

1. Show me a happy face.
2. Show me a sad face.
3. I feel better.
4. I'm excited.
5. I'm calm now.
6. Can I have a hug?

## 替换练习

- I'm happy. / I'm sad. / I'm angry.
- Are you tired? / Are you sleepy?
- Show me a happy face. / Show me a sad face.
- It's okay. / I feel better.
