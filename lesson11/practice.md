# Lesson 11 Practice

## Game 1: Mirror Faces

对着镜子做表情：

- Show me a happy face.
- Show me a sad face.
- I'm angry.

## Game 2: How Do You Feel

家长提问：

- Are you happy?
- Are you sad?
- Are you tired?

## Game 3: Hug Time

在安抚场景里练习：

- It's okay.
- I need a hug.
- Can I have a hug?

## Game 4: Breathe Together

和孩子一起做呼吸：

- Take a deep breath.
- I'm calm now.
- I feel better.

## Game 5: Replace One Feeling

替换练习：

- I'm happy.
- I'm sad.
- I'm excited.

## 两周建议

### Day 1 到 Day 3

只用下面 5 句：

- Are you happy?
- I'm happy.
- Are you sad?
- I'm sad.
- It's okay.

### Day 4 到 Day 7

增加 5 句：

- Are you angry?
- I'm angry.
- Are you tired?
- I'm tired.
- I need a hug.

### Day 8 到 Day 14

加入问答和替换：

- Are you scared?
- I'm scared.
- Are you sleepy?
- Take a deep breath.
- I feel better.
