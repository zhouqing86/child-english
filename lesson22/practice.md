# Lesson 22 Practice

## Game 1: Pass It Politely

递物品练习：

- Please.
- Here you are.
- Thank you.
- You're welcome.

## Game 2: Turn Taking

轮流游戏时练习：

- My turn.
- Your turn.
- Wait your turn.
- After you.

## Game 3: Ask for Help

角色扮演：

- Can you help me?
- Yes, I can.
- No problem.

## Game 4: Door Game

进出门时练习：

- Excuse me.
- May I come in?
- Sorry.

## Game 5: Meet and Greet

和玩偶或家人练习：

- Nice to meet you.
- See you later.
- Let's be polite.

## 两周建议

### Day 1 到 Day 3

只用下面 5 句：

- Please.
- Thank you.
- You're welcome.
- Sorry.
- Excuse me.

### Day 4 到 Day 7

增加 5 句：

- Can I have some water?
- Here you are.
- Can you help me?
- Yes, I can.
- No problem.

### Day 8 到 Day 14

加入问答和替换：

- May I come in?
- Nice to meet you.
- After you.
- My turn.
- Your turn.
