# Lesson 22 Dialogues

## Dialogue 1

Parent: Can I have some water?

Child: Here you are.

Parent: Thank you.

Child: You're welcome.

## Dialogue 2

Parent: Excuse me.

Child: Yes?

Parent: May I come in?

Child: Yes.

## Dialogue 3

Parent: Can you help me?

Child: Yes, I can.

Parent: Thank you.

Child: No problem.

## Dialogue 4

Parent: Nice to meet you.

Child: Nice to meet you.

Parent: See you later.

Child: Bye.
