# Lesson 22 Sentences

## 必须掌握

1. Please.
2. Thank you.
3. You're welcome.
4. Sorry.
5. Excuse me.
6. May I come in?
7. Can I have some water?
8. Here you are.
9. Nice to meet you.
10. Let's share.
11. After you.
12. Good manners.
13. Can you help me?
14. Yes, I can.
15. No problem.
16. See you later.

## 可扩展

1. You're kind.
2. May I sit here?
3. Wait your turn.
4. My turn.
5. Your turn.
6. Let's be polite.

## 替换练习

- Please. / Thank you. / Sorry.
- Can I have some water? / Can you help me?
- My turn. / Your turn.
- Nice to meet you. / See you later.
