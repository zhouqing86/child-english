# Lesson 24 Practice

## Game 1: One-Day Role Play

把一天流程串起来：

- Good morning.
- I want water.
- Put on your shoes.
- Good night.

## Game 2: Pick and Say

从前 23 课里随机抽句子卡：

- Let's wash your hands.
- Look at the tree.
- It's time to eat.
- Brush your teeth.

## Game 3: Home to Park

从家到外出的连续练习：

- Put on your shoes.
- Let's go to the park.
- Time to go home.

## Game 4: Meal and Bedtime

练习日常固定流程：

- I want soup.
- I'm full.
- Take a bath.
- I am sleepy.

## Game 5: Review and Choose

让孩子从多句里挑自己最会说的句子：

- Good morning.
- Thank you.
- Where is my bag?
- Great talking.

## 两周建议

### Day 1 到 Day 3

只复习下面 5 句：

- Good morning.
- I want water.
- Put on your shoes.
- It's time to eat.
- Good night.

### Day 4 到 Day 7

增加 5 句：

- Let's wash your hands.
- Let's go to the park.
- Look at the tree.
- I want soup.
- I'm full.

### Day 8 到 Day 14

加入综合串联：

- Time to go home.
- Take a bath.
- Brush your teeth.
- I am sleepy.
- See you tomorrow.
