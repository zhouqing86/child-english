# Lesson 24 Dialogues

## Dialogue 1

Parent: Good morning.

Child: Good morning.

Parent: Let's wash your hands.

Child: Okay.

## Dialogue 2

Parent: I want water.

Child: Here you are.

Parent: Put on your shoes.

Child: Okay.

## Dialogue 3

Parent: Let's go to the park.

Child: Yay.

Parent: Look at the tree.

Child: Big tree.

Parent: I'm happy.

Child: Me too.

## Dialogue 4

Parent: It's time to eat.

Child: I want soup.

Parent: Are you full?

Child: I'm full.

Parent: Good night.

Child: See you tomorrow.
