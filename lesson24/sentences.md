# Lesson 24 Sentences

## 必须掌握

1. Good morning.
2. Let's wash your hands.
3. I want water.
4. Put on your shoes.
5. Let's go to the park.
6. Look at the tree.
7. I'm happy.
8. It's time to eat.
9. I want soup.
10. I'm full.
11. Time to go home.
12. Take a bath.
13. Brush your teeth.
14. I am sleepy.
15. Good night.
16. See you tomorrow.

## 可扩展

1. Where is my bag?
2. Here it is.
3. Let's clean up.
4. Thank you.
5. Please help me.
6. Great talking.

## 替换练习

- Good morning. / Good night.
- I want water. / I want soup.
- Let's go to the park. / Time to go home.
- Where is my bag? / Here it is.
