# Lesson 12 Dialogues

## Dialogue 1

Parent: Let's check the weather.

Child: Okay.

Parent: It's sunny.

Child: The sun is out.

## Dialogue 2

Parent: Is it rainy?

Child: Yes. It's rainy.

Parent: Take your umbrella.

Child: Okay.

## Dialogue 3

Parent: It's cold.

Child: Brrr.

Parent: Put on a sweater.

Child: Okay.

Parent: We need boots.

## Dialogue 4

Parent: It's hot.

Child: Hot.

Parent: Take off your jacket.

Child: Okay.
