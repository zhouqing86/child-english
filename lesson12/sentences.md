# Lesson 12 Sentences

## 必须掌握

1. It's sunny.
2. It's rainy.
3. It's cloudy.
4. It's windy.
5. It's hot.
6. It's cold.
7. Wear your hat.
8. Wear your raincoat.
9. Take your umbrella.
10. Put on a sweater.
11. Take off your jacket.
12. The sun is out.
13. The rain is falling.
14. Let's check the weather.
15. It's a cold day.
16. We need boots.

## 可扩展

1. It's warm today.
2. It's snowing.
3. Wear your scarf.
4. Put on your gloves.
5. The sky is gray.
6. I like sunny days.

## 替换练习

- It's sunny. / It's rainy. / It's windy.
- It's hot. / It's cold.
- Wear your hat. / Wear your raincoat.
- Put on a sweater. / Take off your jacket.
