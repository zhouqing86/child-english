# Lesson 12 Practice

## Game 1: Window Weather

站在窗边练习：

- It's sunny.
- It's rainy.
- It's cloudy.
- It's windy.

## Game 2: Dress for the Weather

拿衣物和配件练习：

- Wear your hat.
- Wear your raincoat.
- Take your umbrella.
- Put on a sweater.

## Game 3: Hot or Cold

家长提问：

- Is it hot?
- Is it cold?
- It's a cold day.

## Game 4: Weather Basket

准备帽子、雨伞、靴子等物品：

- We need boots.
- Put on your gloves.
- Wear your scarf.

## Game 5: Replace One Word

替换练习：

- It's sunny.
- It's rainy.
- It's windy.

## 两周建议

### Day 1 到 Day 3

只用下面 5 句：

- It's sunny.
- It's rainy.
- It's hot.
- It's cold.
- Let's check the weather.

### Day 4 到 Day 7

增加 5 句：

- It's cloudy.
- It's windy.
- Wear your hat.
- Take your umbrella.
- Put on a sweater.

### Day 8 到 Day 14

加入问答和替换：

- Wear your raincoat.
- Take off your jacket.
- The sun is out.
- The rain is falling.
- We need boots.
