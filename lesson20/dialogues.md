# Lesson 20 Dialogues

## Dialogue 1

Parent: This is a table.

Child: Table.

Parent: Put it on the table.

Child: Okay.

## Dialogue 2

Parent: Open the door.

Child: Okay.

Parent: Close the door.

Child: Okay.

## Dialogue 3

Parent: Turn on the light.

Child: Click.

Parent: The room is bright.

Child: Wow.

## Dialogue 4

Parent: Where is the bag?

Child: It's in the box.

Parent: Good finding.

Child: Yay.
