# Lesson 18 Dialogues

## Dialogue 1

Parent: Let's go shopping.

Child: Okay.

Parent: We need milk.

Child: Milk.

## Dialogue 2

Parent: Which one do you want?

Child: I want this one.

Parent: Do you like this one?

Child: I like this one.

## Dialogue 3

Parent: Put it in the cart.

Child: Okay.

Parent: The cart is full.

Child: Wow.

## Dialogue 4

Parent: How much is it?

Child: Here is the money.

Parent: Thank you.

Child: Bye.
