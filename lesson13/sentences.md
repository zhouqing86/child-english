# Lesson 13 Sentences

## 必须掌握

1. Do you want an apple?
2. I want grapes.
3. This is a banana.
4. Peel the banana.
5. Wash the grapes.
6. Take a strawberry.
7. Bite the apple.
8. It's sweet.
9. It's sour.
10. I like oranges.
11. Have a snack.
12. One for you.
13. One for me.
14. More grapes, please.
15. No, thanks.
16. Yummy snack.

## 可扩展

1. This is a peach.
2. This is a pear.
3. Cut the apple.
4. Share the snack.
5. I want crackers.
6. Let's eat fruit.

## 替换练习

- This is a banana. / This is a peach. / This is a pear.
- It's sweet. / It's sour.
- One for you. / One for me.
- More grapes, please. / No, thanks.
