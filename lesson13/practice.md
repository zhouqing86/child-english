# Lesson 13 Practice

## Game 1: Fruit Basket

拿真实水果练习：

- This is a banana.
- Take a strawberry.
- Bite the apple.

## Game 2: Want or No Thanks

家长轮流递不同水果：

- Do you want an apple?
- I want grapes.
- No, thanks.

## Game 3: Sweet or Sour

尝不同食物后练习：

- It's sweet.
- It's sour.

## Game 4: Share the Snack

练习分食物：

- One for you.
- One for me.
- Share the snack.

## Game 5: Replace One Fruit

替换练习：

- I want grapes.
- I like oranges.
- This is a peach.

## 两周建议

### Day 1 到 Day 3

只用下面 5 句：

- Do you want an apple?
- I want grapes.
- This is a banana.
- Have a snack.
- Yummy snack.

### Day 4 到 Day 7

增加 5 句：

- Peel the banana.
- Wash the grapes.
- Take a strawberry.
- It's sweet.
- It's sour.

### Day 8 到 Day 14

加入问答和替换：

- I like oranges.
- One for you.
- One for me.
- More grapes, please.
- No, thanks.
