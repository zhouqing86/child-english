# Lesson 13 Dialogues

## Dialogue 1

Parent: Do you want an apple?

Child: No, thanks.

Parent: What do you want?

Child: I want grapes.

## Dialogue 2

Parent: This is a banana.

Child: Banana.

Parent: Peel the banana.

Child: Okay.

## Dialogue 3

Parent: Take a strawberry.

Child: Thank you.

Parent: Is it sweet?

Child: It's sweet.

## Dialogue 4

Parent: Have a snack.

Child: Okay.

Parent: More grapes, please?

Child: Yes. More grapes, please.
