# Lesson 15 Practice

## Game 1: Animal Sound Match

用玩具或图片练习：

- This is a dog.
- The dog says woof.
- This is a cat.
- The cat says meow.

## Game 2: Guess the Animal

家长先学动物声音，让孩子猜：

- Which animal is it?
- The duck says quack.
- The cow says moo.

## Game 3: Move Like an Animal

边做动作边说：

- The rabbit hops.
- The fish swims.
- The horse runs.

## Game 4: Favorite Animal

和孩子聊喜欢的动物：

- I like the lion.
- I like the panda.

## Game 5: Replace One Animal

替换练习：

- This is a dog.
- This is a sheep.
- This is a frog.

## 两周建议

### Day 1 到 Day 3

只用下面 5 句：

- This is a dog.
- The dog says woof.
- This is a cat.
- The cat says meow.
- Good animal sound.

### Day 4 到 Day 7

增加 5 句：

- This is a bird.
- The bird says tweet.
- This is a duck.
- The duck says quack.
- Which animal is it?

### Day 8 到 Day 14

加入问答和替换：

- This is a cow.
- The cow says moo.
- The rabbit hops.
- The fish swims.
- I like the lion.
