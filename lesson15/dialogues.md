# Lesson 15 Dialogues

## Dialogue 1

Parent: This is a dog.

Child: Dog.

Parent: The dog says woof.

Child: Woof.

## Dialogue 2

Parent: This is a cat.

Child: Cat.

Parent: The cat says meow.

Child: Meow.

## Dialogue 3

Parent: Which animal is it?

Child: Duck.

Parent: The duck says quack.

Child: Quack.

## Dialogue 4

Parent: I like the lion.

Child: Roar.

Parent: Good animal sound.

Child: Again.
