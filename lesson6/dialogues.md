# Lesson 6 Dialogues

## Dialogue 1

Parent: Let's play.

Child: Okay.

Parent: I want the ball.

Child: Here you are.

Parent: Throw the ball.

Child: Okay.

## Dialogue 2

Parent: Catch the ball.

Child: I got it.

Parent: Good job.

## Dialogue 3

Parent: Build a tower.

Child: Okay.

Parent: It's falling down.

Child: Oh no.

Parent: One more block.

## Dialogue 4

Parent: Pick up your toys.

Child: Okay.

Parent: Put it in the box.

Child: Okay.

Parent: Clean up, please.

Child: The room is clean.
