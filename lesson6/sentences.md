# Lesson 6 Sentences

## 必须掌握

1. This is your toy.
2. Let's play.
3. I want the ball.
4. Throw the ball.
5. Catch the ball.
6. Roll the car.
7. Build a tower.
8. It's falling down.
9. Pick up your toys.
10. Put it in the box.
11. Clean up, please.
12. This is my teddy bear.
13. Where is the puzzle?
14. Here it is.
15. Share your toy.
16. Good cleaning up.

## 可扩展

1. One more block.
2. Let's open the box.
3. Close the box.
4. The room is clean.
5. Let's play together.
6. My toy car.

## 替换练习

- Throw the ball. / Catch the ball.
- Open the box. / Close the box.
- This is your toy. / This is my teddy bear.
- Pick up your toys. / Put it in the box.
