# Lesson 6 Practice

## Game 1: Ball Time

边玩球边说：

- Throw the ball.
- Catch the ball.
- Roll the ball.

## Game 2: Build and Fall

搭积木时练习：

- Build a tower.
- One more block.
- It's falling down.

## Game 3: Find the Toy

家长提问：

- Where is the puzzle?
- Where is your teddy bear?

孩子找到后说 Here it is.

## Game 4: Clean Up Race

收玩具时练习：

- Pick up your toys.
- Put it in the box.
- Clean up, please.

## Game 5: Share It

和家长轮流玩：

- Share your toy.
- My toy car.
- Let's play together.

## 两周建议

### Day 1 到 Day 3

只用下面 5 句：

- Let's play.
- I want the ball.
- Throw the ball.
- Catch the ball.
- Good job.

### Day 4 到 Day 7

增加 5 句：

- Roll the car.
- Build a tower.
- It's falling down.
- Where is the puzzle?
- Here it is.

### Day 8 到 Day 14

加入问答和替换：

- Pick up your toys.
- Put it in the box.
- Clean up, please.
- Share your toy.
- The room is clean.
