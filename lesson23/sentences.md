# Lesson 23 Sentences

## 必须掌握

1. My tummy hurts.
2. My head hurts.
3. I don't feel well.
4. I am sick.
5. I am hot.
6. I am cold.
7. I need water.
8. Please help me.
9. Be careful.
10. Walk slowly.
11. Stop right there.
12. Don't touch that.
13. It's too hot.
14. It's dangerous.
15. Take a rest.
16. Are you okay?

## 可扩展

1. I need a tissue.
2. Cough cough.
3. Achoo.
4. Call Mommy.
5. Sit down, please.
6. Drink some water.

## 替换练习

- My tummy hurts. / My head hurts.
- I am hot. / I am cold.
- Be careful. / Walk slowly.
- It's too hot. / It's dangerous.
