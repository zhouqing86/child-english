# Lesson 23 Dialogues

## Dialogue 1

Parent: Are you okay?

Child: My tummy hurts.

Parent: Oh. Take a rest.

Child: Okay.

## Dialogue 2

Parent: How do you feel?

Child: I don't feel well.

Parent: Please help me?

Child: Please help me.

## Dialogue 3

Parent: Be careful.

Child: Okay.

Parent: Walk slowly.

Child: Okay.

## Dialogue 4

Parent: Don't touch that.

Child: Why?

Parent: It's too hot.

Child: Okay.
