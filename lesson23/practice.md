# Lesson 23 Practice

## Game 1: Doll Doctor

用玩偶角色扮演：

- My tummy hurts.
- My head hurts.
- Take a rest.

## Game 2: Hot or Cold

通过表情和动作练习：

- I am hot.
- I am cold.
- I need water.

## Game 3: Safety Walk

边走边练习：

- Be careful.
- Walk slowly.
- Stop right there.

## Game 4: Safe or Not

指向安全或危险物品练习：

- Don't touch that.
- It's too hot.
- It's dangerous.

## Game 5: Help Me

角色扮演练习：

- Please help me.
- Are you okay?
- Call Mommy.

## 两周建议

### Day 1 到 Day 3

只用下面 5 句：

- My tummy hurts.
- I don't feel well.
- Please help me.
- Be careful.
- Are you okay?

### Day 4 到 Day 7

增加 5 句：

- My head hurts.
- I am hot.
- I am cold.
- Walk slowly.
- Take a rest.

### Day 8 到 Day 14

加入问答和替换：

- I am sick.
- I need water.
- Stop right there.
- Don't touch that.
- It's dangerous.
