# Lesson 5 Sentences

## 必须掌握

1. This is Mommy.
2. This is Daddy.
3. Hi, Mommy.
4. Hi, Daddy.
5. I love Mommy.
6. I love Daddy.
7. Come to Mommy.
8. Come to Daddy.
9. Grandma is here.
10. Grandpa is here.
11. Give Daddy a hug.
12. Give Mommy a kiss.
13. Let's call Grandma.
14. Say hello.
15. Say bye-bye.
16. We are family.

## 可扩展

1. This is my brother.
2. This is my sister.
3. Sit with Mommy.
4. Hold my hand.
5. Thank you, Grandma.
6. I miss Daddy.

## 替换练习

- This is Mommy. / This is Daddy. / This is Grandma.
- I love Mommy. / I love Daddy.
- Come to Mommy. / Come to Daddy.
- Say hello. / Say bye-bye.
