# Lesson 5 Dialogues

## Dialogue 1

Parent: This is Mommy.

Child: Hi, Mommy.

Parent: I love you.

Child: I love Mommy.

## Dialogue 2

Parent: This is Daddy.

Child: Hi, Daddy.

Parent: Come to Daddy.

Child: Okay.

Parent: Give Daddy a hug.

Child: Hug.

## Dialogue 3

Parent: Grandma is here.

Child: Hello, Grandma.

Parent: Grandpa is here.

Child: Hello, Grandpa.

## Dialogue 4

Parent: Let's call Grandma.

Child: Okay.

Parent: Say hello.

Child: Hello.

Parent: Say bye-bye.

Child: Bye-bye.
