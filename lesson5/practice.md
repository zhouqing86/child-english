# Lesson 5 Practice

## Game 1: Family Photo

拿家人照片练习：

- This is Mommy.
- This is Daddy.
- Grandma is here.
- Grandpa is here.

## Game 2: Hug and Kiss

家长说句子，孩子做动作：

- Give Daddy a hug.
- Give Mommy a kiss.
- Hold my hand.

## Game 3: Knock and Call

模仿打电话：

- Let's call Grandma.
- Say hello.
- Say bye-bye.

## Game 4: Come Here

不同家人轮流说：

- Come to Mommy.
- Come to Daddy.

孩子跑向对应家人。

## Game 5: Replace One Name

替换练习：

- I love Mommy.
- I love Daddy.
- Thank you, Grandma.

## 两周建议

### Day 1 到 Day 3

只用下面 5 句：

- This is Mommy.
- This is Daddy.
- Hi, Mommy.
- Hi, Daddy.
- I love Mommy.

### Day 4 到 Day 7

增加 5 句：

- I love Daddy.
- Come to Mommy.
- Come to Daddy.
- Give Daddy a hug.
- Give Mommy a kiss.

### Day 8 到 Day 14

加入问答和替换：

- Grandma is here.
- Grandpa is here.
- Let's call Grandma.
- Say hello.
- Say bye-bye.
