# Lesson 16 Dialogues

## Dialogue 1

Parent: Let's go to the park.

Child: Yay.

Parent: Hold my hand.

Child: Okay.

## Dialogue 2

Parent: Look at the tree.

Child: Big tree.

Parent: Look at the flower.

Child: Pretty.

## Dialogue 3

Parent: Go down the slide.

Child: Whee.

Parent: Sit on the swing.

Child: Push me, please.

## Dialogue 4

Parent: It's a nice day.

Child: Yes.

Parent: Time to go home.

Child: Bye, park.
