# Lesson 16 Practice

## Game 1: Park Walk

散步时练习：

- Hold my hand.
- Walk with me.
- Let's walk slowly.

## Game 2: Look and Find

在公园里找东西：

- Look at the tree.
- Look at the flower.
- I see a bird.

## Game 3: Playground Actions

玩滑梯和秋千时练习：

- Go up the stairs.
- Go down the slide.
- Sit on the swing.
- Push me, please.

## Game 4: Fast or Slow

跑步和走路时练习：

- Run on the grass.
- Let's walk fast.
- Let's walk slowly.

## Game 5: Home Time

离开前练习：

- Stop by the bench.
- Time to go home.
- Bye, park.

## 两周建议

### Day 1 到 Day 3

只用下面 5 句：

- Let's go to the park.
- Hold my hand.
- Look at the tree.
- I see a bird.
- It's a nice day.

### Day 4 到 Day 7

增加 5 句：

- Walk with me.
- Look at the flower.
- Go up the stairs.
- Go down the slide.
- Sit on the swing.

### Day 8 到 Day 14

加入问答和替换：

- Push me, please.
- Run on the grass.
- Stop by the bench.
- Time to go home.
- Bye, park.
