# Lesson 16 Sentences

## 必须掌握

1. Let's go to the park.
2. Hold my hand.
3. Walk with me.
4. Look at the tree.
5. Look at the flower.
6. I see a bird.
7. The bird is flying.
8. Go up the stairs.
9. Go down the slide.
10. Sit on the swing.
11. Push me, please.
12. Run on the grass.
13. Stop by the bench.
14. It's a nice day.
15. Time to go home.
16. Bye, park.

## 可扩展

1. Look at the cloud.
2. I see a dog.
3. Let's walk slowly.
4. Let's walk fast.
5. The leaves are green.
6. We are outside.

## 替换练习

- Look at the tree. / Look at the flower. / Look at the cloud.
- Go up the stairs. / Go down the slide.
- Let's walk slowly. / Let's walk fast.
- I see a bird. / I see a dog.
