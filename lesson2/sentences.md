# Lesson 2 Sentences

## 必须掌握

1. Do you want water?
2. I want water.
3. Here you are.
4. Drink some water.
5. I'm thirsty.
6. Do you want milk?
7. I want milk.
8. Let's eat.
9. I'm hungry.
10. I want an apple.
11. Take a bite.
12. Chew slowly.
13. More, please.
14. No more.
15. It's yummy.
16. Thank you.

## 可扩展

1. I want a banana.
2. I want bread.
3. Sit down, please.
4. Use your spoon.
5. Wipe your mouth.
6. This is for you.

## 替换练习

- I want water. / I want milk. / I want bread.
- Do you want water? / Do you want milk?
- I want an apple. / I want a banana.
- More, please. / No more.
