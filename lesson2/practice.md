# Lesson 2 Practice

## Game 1: Drink or Eat

家长说句子，孩子做动作：

- Drink some water.
- Let's eat.
- Take a bite.
- Chew slowly.
- Wipe your mouth.

## Game 2: Choice

家长提问：

- Do you want water?
- Do you want milk?
- Do you want an apple?

孩子可以指物、点头，或直接说完整句。

## Game 3: Ask for More

准备少量水果或饼干，让孩子练习：

- More, please.
- No more.
- Thank you.

## Game 4: Replace One Word

替换练习：

- I want water.
- I want milk.
- I want bread.

## Game 5: Feed the Toy

用玩具小熊或娃娃扮演吃饭：

- Teddy is hungry.
- Teddy wants water.
- Here you are.

## 两周建议

### Day 1 到 Day 3

只用下面 5 句：

- Do you want water?
- I want water.
- Here you are.
- Let's eat.
- Thank you.

### Day 4 到 Day 7

增加 5 句：

- I'm thirsty.
- Do you want milk?
- I want milk.
- I'm hungry.
- Take a bite.

### Day 8 到 Day 14

加入替换和问答：

- I want an apple.
- More, please.
- No more.
- It's yummy.
- Wipe your mouth.
