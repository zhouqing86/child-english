# Lesson 2 Dialogues

## Dialogue 1

Parent: Do you want water?

Child: I want water.

Parent: Here you are.

Child: Thank you.

## Dialogue 2

Parent: Do you want milk?

Child: I want milk.

Parent: Drink some milk.

Child: Okay.

## Dialogue 3

Parent: Let's eat.

Child: Okay.

Parent: Are you hungry?

Child: I'm hungry.

Parent: Take a bite.

Child: It's yummy.

## Dialogue 4

Parent: Do you want more?

Child: More, please.

Parent: Here you are.

Child: Thank you.

Parent: No more?

Child: No more.
