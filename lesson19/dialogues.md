# Lesson 19 Dialogues

## Dialogue 1

Parent: It's morning.

Child: Good morning.

Parent: First wash your hands.

Child: Okay.

## Dialogue 2

Parent: It's time to eat.

Child: Yay.

Parent: Then let's eat.

Child: Okay.

## Dialogue 3

Parent: Now it's bath time.

Child: Splash.

Parent: Next brush your teeth.

Child: Okay.

## Dialogue 4

Parent: What time is it?

Child: It's bedtime.

Parent: Later we will read.

Child: Yay.
