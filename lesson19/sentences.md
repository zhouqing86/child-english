# Lesson 19 Sentences

## 必须掌握

1. It's morning.
2. It's afternoon.
3. It's night.
4. It's time to eat.
5. It's time to play.
6. It's time to sleep.
7. First wash your hands.
8. Then let's eat.
9. Next brush your teeth.
10. Now it's bath time.
11. Later we will read.
12. What time is it?
13. It's bedtime.
14. Hurry up.
15. Wait a minute.
16. Good routine.

## 可扩展

1. Before dinner.
2. After dinner.
3. Today is busy.
4. Tomorrow we will play.
5. First this, then that.
6. It's time to go.

## 替换练习

- It's morning. / It's afternoon. / It's night.
- It's time to eat. / It's time to play. / It's time to sleep.
- First wash your hands. / Next brush your teeth.
- Hurry up. / Wait a minute.
