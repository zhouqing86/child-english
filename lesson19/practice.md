# Lesson 19 Practice

## Game 1: Daily Order Cards

用起床、吃饭、洗澡、睡觉图片排序：

- First wash your hands.
- Then let's eat.
- Next brush your teeth.

## Game 2: Time Match

对照真实时间点练习：

- It's morning.
- It's afternoon.
- It's night.

## Game 3: Now or Later

家长提问：

- Now it's bath time.
- Later we will read.
- It's time to sleep.

## Game 4: Hurry or Wait

在不同节奏活动里练习：

- Hurry up.
- Wait a minute.

## Game 5: Replace One Time Word

替换练习：

- It's time to eat.
- It's time to play.
- It's time to sleep.

## 两周建议

### Day 1 到 Day 3

只用下面 5 句：

- It's morning.
- It's time to eat.
- It's time to play.
- It's time to sleep.
- Good routine.

### Day 4 到 Day 7

增加 5 句：

- It's afternoon.
- It's night.
- First wash your hands.
- Then let's eat.
- Next brush your teeth.

### Day 8 到 Day 14

加入问答和替换：

- Now it's bath time.
- Later we will read.
- What time is it?
- It's bedtime.
- Wait a minute.
