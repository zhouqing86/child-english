# Lesson 8 Practice

## Game 1: Count Toys

拿玩具练习：

- One apple.
- Two bananas.
- Three blocks.
- Four cars.

## Game 2: Give Me

家长发指令：

- Give me one.
- Give me two.
- One more.

## Game 3: How Many

家长摆出物品提问：

- How many?
- I see one.
- I see two.

## Game 4: Finger Count

用手指练习：

- One finger.
- Two fingers.
- Ten fingers.

## Game 5: Count Again

把物品重新摆好，重复：

- Let's count again.
- Good counting.

## 两周建议

### Day 1 到 Day 3

只用下面 5 句：

- Count with me.
- One apple.
- Two bananas.
- How many?
- Good counting.

### Day 4 到 Day 7

增加 5 句：

- Three blocks.
- Four cars.
- Five ducks.
- I see one.
- Give me one.

### Day 8 到 Day 14

加入问答和替换：

- I see two.
- Give me two.
- One more.
- No more.
- Let's count again.
