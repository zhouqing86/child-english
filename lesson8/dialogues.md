# Lesson 8 Dialogues

## Dialogue 1

Parent: Count with me.

Child: Okay.

Parent: One, two, three.

Child: One, two, three.

## Dialogue 2

Parent: How many apples?

Child: One apple.

Parent: Good.

## Dialogue 3

Parent: Give me two blocks.

Child: Here you are.

Parent: Thank you.

## Dialogue 4

Parent: Do you want one more?

Child: One more.

Parent: Let's count again.

Child: Okay.
