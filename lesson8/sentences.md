# Lesson 8 Sentences

## 必须掌握

1. Count with me.
2. One apple.
3. Two bananas.
4. Three blocks.
5. Four cars.
6. Five ducks.
7. One, two, three.
8. How many?
9. I see one.
10. I see two.
11. Give me one.
12. Give me two.
13. One more.
14. No more.
15. Let's count again.
16. Good counting.

## 可扩展

1. Six balls.
2. Seven books.
3. Eight cups.
4. Nine stars.
5. Ten fingers.
6. There are three.

## 替换练习

- One apple. / Two bananas. / Three blocks.
- Give me one. / Give me two.
- I see one. / I see two.
- One more. / No more.
