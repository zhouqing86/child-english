# Lesson 1 Practice

## Game 1: Do It

家长说句子，孩子做动作：

- Wake up.
- Brush your teeth.
- Put on your socks.
- Open the door.
- Come here, please.

## Game 2: Find It

家长提问：

- Where are your shoes?
- Where is your shirt?

孩子用手指出或拿出来。

## Game 3: Repeat and Hug

家长说：

- Give me a hug.
- Good morning.
- I'm ready.

孩子模仿后给予拥抱或表扬。

## Game 4: Choice

家长提问：

- Are you sleepy?
- Are you ready?
- Do you want water?

孩子可以点头、摇头、说 yes 或 no，也可以尝试完整回答。

## Game 5: Replace One Word

替换练习：

- Put on your shirt.
- Put on your socks.
- Put on your shoes.

## 两周建议

### Day 1 到 Day 3

只用下面 5 句：

- Good morning.
- Wake up.
- Time to get up.
- Brush your teeth.
- Let's go.

### Day 4 到 Day 7

增加 5 句：

- Put on your shirt.
- Put on your socks.
- Where are your shoes?
- Here they are.
- Are you ready?

### Day 8 到 Day 14

加入问答和替换：

- I'm ready.
- I want water.
- I'm thirsty.
- Open the door.
- Give me a hug.
