# Lesson 1 Sentences

## 必须掌握

1. Good morning.
2. Wake up.
3. Time to get up.
4. Are you sleepy?
5. Let's wash your face.
6. Brush your teeth.
7. Put on your shirt.
8. Put on your socks.
9. Put on your shoes.
10. Where are your shoes?
11. Here they are.
12. Come here, please.
13. Are you ready?
14. I'm ready.
15. Open the door.
16. Let's go.

## 可扩展

1. I want water.
2. I'm thirsty.
3. This is your shirt.
4. Hurry up.
5. Give me a hug.
6. Good job.

## 替换练习

- Put on your shirt. / Put on your socks. / Put on your shoes.
- Are you sleepy? / Are you ready?
- I want water. / I want milk.
- Here they are. / Here you are.
