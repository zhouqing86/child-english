# Lesson 1 Dialogues

## Dialogue 1

Parent: Good morning.

Child: Good morning.

Parent: Wake up.

Child: Okay.

Parent: Time to get up.

Child: Okay.

## Dialogue 2

Parent: Are you sleepy?

Child: Yes.

Parent: Let's wash your face.

Child: Okay.

Parent: Brush your teeth.

Child: Okay.

## Dialogue 3

Parent: Put on your shirt.

Child: Okay.

Parent: Put on your socks.

Child: Okay.

Parent: Where are your shoes?

Child: Here they are.

## Dialogue 4

Parent: Are you ready?

Child: I'm ready.

Parent: Open the door.

Child: Okay.

Parent: Let's go.

Child: Let's go.
