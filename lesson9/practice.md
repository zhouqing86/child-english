# Lesson 9 Practice

## Game 1: Simon Says Body

家长发指令：

- Touch your head.
- Touch your nose.
- Touch your ears.
- Show me your hands.

## Game 2: Clap and Stamp

边做动作边说：

- Clap your hands.
- Stamp your feet.
- Wiggle your fingers.

## Game 3: Point Fast

快速指身体部位：

- Point to your nose.
- Point to your mouth.
- Show me your tummy.

## Game 4: Mirror Game

家长和孩子互相模仿：

- Raise your arms.
- Bend your knees.
- Move your toes.

## Game 5: Replace One Part

替换练习：

- Touch your head.
- Touch your eyes.
- Touch your mouth.

## 两周建议

### Day 1 到 Day 3

只用下面 5 句：

- Touch your head.
- Touch your nose.
- Show me your hands.
- Clap your hands.
- Good listening.

### Day 4 到 Day 7

增加 5 句：

- Touch your eyes.
- Touch your mouth.
- Show me your feet.
- Stamp your feet.
- Raise your arms.

### Day 8 到 Day 14

加入问答和替换：

- Wiggle your fingers.
- Bend your knees.
- Show me your tummy.
- Point to your nose.
- Point to your mouth.
