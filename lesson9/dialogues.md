# Lesson 9 Dialogues

## Dialogue 1

Parent: Touch your head.

Child: Like this.

Parent: Good.

## Dialogue 2

Parent: Touch your nose.

Child: Here.

Parent: Point to your mouth.

Child: Here.

## Dialogue 3

Parent: Show me your hands.

Child: Here.

Parent: Clap your hands.

Child: Clap clap.

## Dialogue 4

Parent: Raise your arms.

Child: Okay.

Parent: Stamp your feet.

Child: Boom boom.

Parent: Good listening.

Child: Yay.
