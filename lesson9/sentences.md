# Lesson 9 Sentences

## 必须掌握

1. Touch your head.
2. Touch your eyes.
3. Touch your nose.
4. Touch your mouth.
5. Touch your ears.
6. Show me your hands.
7. Show me your feet.
8. Clap your hands.
9. Stamp your feet.
10. Wiggle your fingers.
11. Bend your knees.
12. Raise your arms.
13. Show me your tummy.
14. Point to your nose.
15. Point to your mouth.
16. Good listening.

## 可扩展

1. Touch your shoulders.
2. Touch your legs.
3. Shake your hands.
4. Move your toes.
5. This is my body.
6. My eyes are open.

## 替换练习

- Touch your head. / Touch your nose. / Touch your ears.
- Show me your hands. / Show me your feet.
- Point to your nose. / Point to your mouth.
- Clap your hands. / Stamp your feet.
