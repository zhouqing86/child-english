# Lesson 14 Practice

## Game 1: Table Setup

摆餐具时练习：

- This is your bowl.
- Put it on the table.
- Use your spoon.

## Game 2: Meal Choice

家长提问：

- Do you want soup?
- I want soup.
- Pass the rice, please.

## Game 3: Eat and Drink

吃饭时练习：

- Eat your noodles.
- Drink your milk.
- Here is your egg.

## Game 4: Done or More

餐中或餐后练习：

- I want more soup.
- I'm full.
- All done.

## Game 5: Clean the Table

收尾时练习：

- Wipe the table.
- The bowl is empty.
- Thank you for dinner.

## 两周建议

### Day 1 到 Day 3

只用下面 5 句：

- It's breakfast time.
- Sit at the table.
- Use your spoon.
- Drink your milk.
- Thank you for dinner.

### Day 4 到 Day 7

增加 5 句：

- Let's eat rice.
- Eat your noodles.
- Here is your egg.
- Do you want soup?
- I want soup.

### Day 8 到 Day 14

加入问答和替换：

- Use your fork.
- Put it on the table.
- I'm full.
- All done.
- Wipe the table.
