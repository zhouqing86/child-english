# Lesson 14 Dialogues

## Dialogue 1

Parent: It's breakfast time.

Child: Okay.

Parent: Sit at the table.

Child: Okay.

## Dialogue 2

Parent: Do you want soup?

Child: I want soup.

Parent: Here you are.

Child: Thank you.

## Dialogue 3

Parent: Use your spoon.

Child: Okay.

Parent: Drink your milk.

Child: Okay.

## Dialogue 4

Parent: Are you full?

Child: I'm full.

Parent: All done?

Child: All done.

Parent: Thank you for dinner.
