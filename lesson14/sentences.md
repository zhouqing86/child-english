# Lesson 14 Sentences

## 必须掌握

1. It's breakfast time.
2. Let's eat rice.
3. Eat your noodles.
4. Here is your egg.
5. Do you want soup?
6. I want soup.
7. Use your spoon.
8. Use your fork.
9. This is your bowl.
10. Put it on the table.
11. Sit at the table.
12. Drink your milk.
13. I'm full.
14. All done.
15. Wipe the table.
16. Thank you for dinner.

## 可扩展

1. This is breakfast.
2. This is lunch.
3. This is dinner.
4. Pass the rice, please.
5. I want more soup.
6. The bowl is empty.

## 替换练习

- It's breakfast time. / This is dinner.
- Use your spoon. / Use your fork.
- I want soup. / I want more soup.
- I'm full. / All done.
