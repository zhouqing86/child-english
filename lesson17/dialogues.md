# Lesson 17 Dialogues

## Dialogue 1

Parent: This is a bus.

Child: Bus.

Parent: Wait for the bus.

Child: Okay.

## Dialogue 2

Parent: Get in the car.

Child: Okay.

Parent: Buckle up.

Child: Click.

## Dialogue 3

Parent: Look, a train.

Child: Fast train.

Parent: The train is coming.

Child: Wow.

## Dialogue 4

Parent: The light is red.

Child: Stop.

Parent: The light is green.

Child: Let's go home.
