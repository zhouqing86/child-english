# Lesson 21 Dialogues

## Dialogue 1

Parent: Where is my toy?

Child: Hmm.

Parent: Look in the bag.

Child: Not there.

## Dialogue 2

Parent: Look under the table.

Child: I found it.

Parent: Good finding.

## Dialogue 3

Parent: Where is the book?

Child: It is on the shelf.

Parent: Good.

## Dialogue 4

Parent: Can you find it?

Child: Yes.

Parent: Where is it?

Child: It is behind the door.
