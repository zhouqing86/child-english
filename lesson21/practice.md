# Lesson 21 Practice

## Game 1: Hide and Find

藏玩具练习：

- It is in the box.
- It is under the chair.
- Can you find it?

## Game 2: Look Here

家长发提示：

- Look under the table.
- Look in the bag.
- Look behind the sofa.

## Game 3: Say the Place

找到物品后练习：

- It is on the table.
- It is next to the bed.
- It is on the shelf.

## Game 4: Left and Right

加入方向：

- Look on the left.
- Look on the right.

## Game 5: Replace One Position

替换练习：

- It is in the box.
- It is outside the box.
- It is in front of the box.

## 两周建议

### Day 1 到 Day 3

只用下面 5 句：

- It is in the box.
- It is on the table.
- It is under the chair.
- Where is my toy?
- Here it is.

### Day 4 到 Day 7

增加 5 句：

- It is behind the door.
- It is next to the bed.
- Look under the table.
- Look in the bag.
- I found it.

### Day 8 到 Day 14

加入问答和替换：

- Look behind the sofa.
- Can you find it?
- It is on the shelf.
- It is in front of the box.
- It is between the books.
