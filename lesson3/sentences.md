# Lesson 3 Sentences

## 必须掌握

1. Wash your hands.
2. Show me your hands.
3. Your hands are dirty.
4. Use some soap.
5. Rub your hands.
6. Rinse your hands.
7. Dry your hands.
8. Let's brush your teeth.
9. Open your mouth.
10. Brush up and down.
11. Spit it out.
12. Wash your face.
13. Close your eyes.
14. Wipe your face.
15. All clean.
16. Good job.

## 可扩展

1. Here is your toothbrush.
2. This is toothpaste.
3. One more time.
4. Don't touch that.
5. Let's be clean.
6. Nice and clean.

## 替换练习

- Wash your hands. / Wash your face.
- Rub your hands. / Dry your hands.
- Open your mouth. / Close your eyes.
- All clean. / Nice and clean.
