# Lesson 3 Dialogues

## Dialogue 1

Parent: Show me your hands.

Child: Here.

Parent: Your hands are dirty.

Child: Oh.

Parent: Wash your hands.

Child: Okay.

## Dialogue 2

Parent: Use some soap.

Child: Okay.

Parent: Rub your hands.

Child: Like this?

Parent: Yes. Rinse your hands.

## Dialogue 3

Parent: Let's brush your teeth.

Child: Okay.

Parent: Open your mouth.

Child: Ah.

Parent: Brush up and down.

Child: Okay.

## Dialogue 4

Parent: Wash your face.

Child: Okay.

Parent: Close your eyes.

Child: Okay.

Parent: Wipe your face.

Child: All clean.
