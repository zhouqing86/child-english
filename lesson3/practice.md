# Lesson 3 Practice

## Game 1: Do It in the Bathroom

家长说句子，孩子做动作：

- Wash your hands.
- Rub your hands.
- Dry your hands.
- Open your mouth.
- Wipe your face.

## Game 2: Clean or Dirty

家长提问：

- Are your hands dirty?
- Is your face clean?

孩子可以点头、摇头，或说 dirty / clean。

## Game 3: Brush Together

和孩子一起刷牙，重复：

- Let's brush your teeth.
- Open your mouth.
- Brush up and down.

## Game 4: Replace One Word

替换练习：

- Wash your hands.
- Wash your face.
- Dry your hands.

## Game 5: Clean the Doll

给玩偶洗脸洗手：

- Wash the baby.
- Wipe the face.
- All clean.

## 两周建议

### Day 1 到 Day 3

只用下面 5 句：

- Wash your hands.
- Use some soap.
- Rinse your hands.
- Dry your hands.
- Good job.

### Day 4 到 Day 7

增加 5 句：

- Let's brush your teeth.
- Open your mouth.
- Brush up and down.
- Spit it out.
- All clean.

### Day 8 到 Day 14

加入问答和替换：

- Your hands are dirty.
- Wash your face.
- Close your eyes.
- Wipe your face.
- Nice and clean.
